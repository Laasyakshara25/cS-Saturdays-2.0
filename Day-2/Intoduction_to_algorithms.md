
# Introduction to Algorithms


## **What is an Algorithm?**

An **algorithm** is a well-defined sequence of steps or instructions designed to perform a specific task or solve a problem. It takes input, processes it through a logical sequence, and produces the desired output. Algorithms can be implemented in various programming languages and are fundamental to computer science and problem-solving.



## **Why Are Algorithms Important?**

Algorithms play a crucial role in solving complex problems efficiently. They help us:
- **Optimize Solutions**: Provide faster and more efficient ways to solve problems, reducing time and space complexity.
- **Standardize Approaches**: Offer reusable patterns to tackle similar challenges.
- **Enhance Problem-Solving Skills**: Encourage structured thinking and logical analysis.
- **Enable Scalability**: Ensure that solutions can handle large inputs or datasets effectively.

Understanding and mastering algorithms are essential for competitive programming, technical interviews, and real-world applications.
